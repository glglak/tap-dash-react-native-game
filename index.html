Tap Dash

A simple, addictive endless runner game

About the Game

Tap Dash is a fast-paced, addictive endless runner game where your reflexes and timing are put to the test. Jump over obstacles, collect coins, and see how far you can go!

With simple one-touch controls, anyone can play, but mastering the double-jump technique and timing will take practice. Challenge yourself to beat your high score and unlock achievements as you play.

Simple Controls

Tap to jump, double-tap for a second jump in the air. Easy to learn, challenging to master!

Dynamic Difficulty

The game gets progressively faster and more challenging as your score increases.

Changing Environments

Every 10 points, the background theme changes to keep the gameplay fresh and exciting.
