Privacy Policy - Tap Dash Game

Last updated: March 15, 2025

Welcome to Tap Dash, a simple and fun mobile game. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Don't Collect

Our application is designed with your privacy in mind. Tap Dash does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information about you. We do not:

  • Collect any personal data such as your name, email address, or location
  • Track your device information
  • Use cookies or similar tracking technologies
  • Monitor your activity outside of the basic game functionality
  • Share any information with third parties

Local Storage

Tap Dash uses local storage on your device solely to save your game progress, high scores, and in-game preferences. This information remains on your device and is not transmitted to us or any third parties.

Analytics

We do not use any third-party analytics services to collect information about how you interact with the app.

Advertisements

Tap Dash does not display any advertisements within the application.

Children's Privacy

Our application does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The game is suitable for all ages, and we are committed to protecting the privacy of children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
